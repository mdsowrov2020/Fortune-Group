Fortune Group

A long time ago, in this very land, some renewable Energy Specialists realized that it was time that a company needed to erupt into the existing market and change the laws of the marketplace. Most of those individuals let down that dream due to reasons unknown. But one of them never stopped dreaming. And as thoughts become things, dreams become reality, so inevitable the dream dreamt by that single, pioneering individual, whom we call Md Arifur Rahman, culminated in the birth of Fortune International. The Company was found on a sunny day in 2009 in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The name Fortunes speaks for itself, but just to clarify, the belief of the company stands on the fact that “Fortunes is the unique reality of human”. Periodically Md. Arifur Rahman transformed Fortune International into Fortune's Group in his Business foresight role.
